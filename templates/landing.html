The Nameplate

The Intelligent Nameplate replaces the traditional paper name tent with a connected in-classroom device. Students sign in with a simple tap of their student card, while attendance, participation, polls, quizzes, and engagement are captured automatically. Professors spend less time managing logistics and more time teaching.

Live Attendance

Attendance is recorded the moment a student taps in. No roll call, paper sign-in sheets, or manual tracking.

Participation Grading

Capture meaningful participation with customizable instructor and peer assessments that align with your course and grading policy.

Polls & Quizzing

Send polls and quizzes to every nameplate instantly and watch responses appear live as students answer.

Automatic Grading

Participation, polls, and quizzes are graded automatically, with results ready as soon as class ends.

Real-Time Dashboard

Monitor attendance, raised hands, engagement, and classroom activity from one live dashboard.

Gradebook Export

Export a complete weighted gradebook in a single click, ready to import into your existing LMS or grading system.

About

Comet began with a simple observation: the students who consistently prepare, participate, and engage aren't always recognized fairly, especially in larger classrooms.

We built Comet to make classroom engagement measurable, transparent, and effortless. By connecting attendance, participation, assessments, and classroom interaction into a single platform, Comet gives educators better insight while creating a more engaging experience for students.

This is the first step toward a more connected learning environment.
